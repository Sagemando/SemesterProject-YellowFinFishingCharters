What We Offer

Inshore Fishing

Our inshore fishing charters explore the beautiful backwaters, grass flats, mangrove shorelines, and estuaries of Tampa Bay and the surrounding St. Pete area. Led by Captain King — the #1 inshore captain in Tampa Bay — these trips are perfect for anglers of all experience levels looking to experience the thrill of light tackle fishing in Florida's most productive inshore waters.

Inshore trips typically target:

  • Snook — Captain King's specialty
  • Redfish (Red Drum)
  • Spotted Sea Trout
  • Tarpon
  • Flounder
  • Sheepshead
  • Mangrove Snapper

All rods, reels, tackle, bait, and fishing licenses are included. Trips are available in the morning and afternoon. Party sizes of up to 4 guests are accommodated comfortably.

Offshore Fishing

Our offshore fishing charters venture into the deep, blue waters of the Gulf of Mexico, departing from the St. Pete and Tampa Bay area. Led by Captain Baker Mayfield during the NFL offseason, these trips take you 20 to 80+ miles offshore to where the big fish roam. Whether you're chasing monstrous speedsters or bottom dwellers, our offshore charters deliver an adrenaline-packed deep sea experience unlike any other.

Offshore trips typically target:

  • Yellowfin & Blackfin Tuna — Captain Baker's specialty
  • Mahi-Mahi (Dolphin Fish)
  • Wahoo
  • Kingfish (King Mackerel)
  • Grouper (Red & Gag)
  • Amberjack
  • Red Snapper

All heavy tackle, rods, reels, bait, and fishing licenses are included. Full-day and half-day offshore trips are available. Party sizes of up to 6 guests are accommodated.
