Who We Are

We are your local, St. Pete based, fishing charter company Yellow-Fin Fishing! Our company was born in 2024 from a family with deep ties to the sport of fishing. Our family has been fishing Tampa Bay waters for sport and recreation spanning decades, we finally have the opportunity to deliver our knowledge and passion of fishing to you! We offer full inshore & offshore chartering services. We understand that the hobby of fishing can be expensive, so we also offer rental fishing gear and kayak rentals for all anglers.

What We Offer

We offer a variety of products and services to best suit the needs of all anglers! Our main focus is our inshore and offshore chartering services. Here we can provide you with the best fishing experience without the stress of locating the fish. We provide a range of fishing gear for purchase or rental, this includes rods & reel combos, pre-packaged tackle boxes with in season lures, and more! We're also excited to announce our newly rentable kayaks! We are offering solo, tandem, paddle and pedal powered with plans to expand more in the future!

Mission Statement

We believe the enjoyment of fishing must be accompanied by the preservation of the land and animals we hunt. As anglers it is our responsibility to preserve the beautiful creatures we pull from the lake or sea, and the habitat they reside in. Our company prides itself on providing the best experience for our clients while also standing behind the animals and environment any way we can.
