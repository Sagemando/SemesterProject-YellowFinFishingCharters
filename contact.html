FAQ

  • What should I bring?

    We recommend bringing sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, snacks, and plenty of water. Comfortable, non-slip footwear is also strongly advised.

  • When should I arrive?

    Please arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled departure time to allow for boarding and a safety briefing.

  • What should I wear?

    Light, breathable clothing is best. We recommend layers as mornings on the water can be cool. Avoid open-toed shoes on the boat.

  • What can I expect?

    Expect a fun, guided fishing experience led by our experienced captains. We will provide all necessary equipment unless you have opted out of gear rental.

  • Do I need a fishing license?

    No — your fishing license is covered under our charter license for the duration of your trip. For more information on Florida fishing regulations, visit the Florida Fish & Wildlife Conservation Commission.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    Cancellations made 48 hours or more in advance are fully refunded. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a partial fee. Please contact us directly for more details.
